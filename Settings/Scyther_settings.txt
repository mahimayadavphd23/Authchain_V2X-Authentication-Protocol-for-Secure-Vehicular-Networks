Verification Remarks
----------------------------------------

The above configuration was used to formally verify the proposed AuthChain-V2X
authentication and key agreement protocol. The selected settings provide an
effective balance between verification depth and computational efficiency for
blockchain-assisted V2X communication environments.

Typed matching ensures strict type consistency during message verification,
thereby reducing false attack traces caused by type confusion. The bounded
protocol runs enable systematic analysis of replay attacks, impersonation
attempts, session-mixup attacks, and concurrency-based adversarial behaviour
under realistic communication scenarios.

The verification process validates important security properties of the protocol,
including aliveness, authentication agreement, synchronisation, and session-key
secrecy.
